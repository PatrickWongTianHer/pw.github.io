<h2>
<i><b>Ingredients:</i></b></font>
</h2>

<UL type=circle>
<LI>1 small onion, grade and ginger, chopped</LI>
<LI>3 tablespoons curry powder</LI>
<LI>1 tablespoon ground cinnamon</LI>
<LI>2 skinless, boneless chicken breast</LI>
<LI>1 tablespoon tomato paste</LI>
<LI>Half cup coconut milk</LI>
</UL>
<br>

<font color=blue>
<h3>
<i><b>Steps:</i></b>
</font>
</h3>

<DL>
<DT> Step 1- Ingredients preparation</DT>

<OL type=1 start=2>
<LI> Seal the chicken in a pan with a little oil</LI>
<LI>Add in chopped onion, garlic and ginger</LI>

<OL type=a>
<LI> Cook for 5 minutes to soften</LI>
<LI> Add in curry powder and cinnamon</LI>

<UL type = disc>
<li> Stir and cook for a further minute</LI>
</UL>
</OL>

<LI> Add in tomato paste and coconut milk</LI>
<LI>Simmer for 10 minutes - until the chicken is cooked through</LI>
</OL>
