Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where is Southern Tiger Institute located?

Our main campus is in Johor Bahru. We are easy to reach by car, bus, or train. The area is safe and student-friendly.

2. How are the courses assessed?

Each course has different types of assessment such as exams, assignments, group projects, or presentations. The details are explained in your course guide.

3. What are the admission requirements for international students?

International students need to have good academic results, and a minimum English language score (MUET, IELTS, or TOEFL). More info is available on our Admissions page.

4. Can I use my diploma or degree from another country?

Yes. You need to send your transcript and certificate. We will check and evaluate them before giving you an offer.

5. Is there accommodation for students?

Yes, we have residential colleges for single students and a few family apartments for married students. Please contact our Student Affairs office to apply.

6. What sports or clubs can I join?

You can enjoy football, swimming, tennis, badminton, and more. We also have student clubs for music, debate, innovation, and culture.

7. What programs are offered at Southern Tiger Institute?

We currently offer two programs which is Accounting and Engineering. We are preparing for more more programs in the future such as Computer Science, Business, Interior Design and more. Stay Tuned!

8. How can I apply?

You can apply online through our website. Just fill in the form and upload your documents.

9. How much are the tuition fees?

Fees depend on your program. You will find the fee structure in the official offer letter or on our Courses page.

10. When will I know if I am accepted?

After applying online, you will receive a response within a few working days. If accepted, we will email you the official offer letter.
