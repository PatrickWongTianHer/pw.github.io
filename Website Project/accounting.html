Program Overview

This program prepares students for a professional career in accounting and finance. Students will gain technical knowledge, analytical skills, and business insights.

Entry Requirements

  • ✅ Minimum of 5 credits in SPM including Mathematics and English
  • ✅ STPM / Foundation / Diploma or equivalent qualification
  • ✅ MUET Band 3 or IELTS 5.5 (for international students)

Key Modules

  • Financial Accounting
  • Management Accounting
  • Taxation & Auditing
  • Corporate Finance
  • Business Law & Ethics

Career Opportunities

  • Accountant
  • Financial Analyst
  • Auditor
  • Tax Consultant
  • Budget Planner
