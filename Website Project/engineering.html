Program Overview

This program covers core engineering principles, design thinking, and real-world applications to prepare students for engineering careers in various industries.

Entry Requirements

  • ✅ Minimum of 5 credits in SPM including Mathematics and Physics
  • ✅ STPM / Foundation / Diploma or equivalent qualification
  • ✅ MUET Band 3 or IELTS 5.5 (for international students)

Key Modules

  • Engineering Mathematics
  • Mechanics & Thermodynamics
  • Electrical & Electronic Systems
  • Materials & Manufacturing
  • Capstone Design Project

Career Opportunities

  • Electrical Engineer
  • Civil Engineer
  • Project Manager
  • R&D Engineer
